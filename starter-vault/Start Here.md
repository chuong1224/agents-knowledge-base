# Start Here

Welcome to your first **knowledge vault**. Here is the entire secret up front:

> **A vault is just a folder of plain text files ending in `.md`.**
> No database, no account, no special app. You can open these files with Notepad.

This starter vault has 8 short notes that teach you the basics — by being the thing they teach. Read them in order, in this app's Reader (click the notes in the left sidebar, or press `Ctrl+P` and type a name):

2. [[Linking Notes]] — the `[[double bracket]]` trick that creates the graph
3. [[My First Note]] — yours to edit; get your hands dirty
4. [[Tags and Colors]] — how notes get their colors on the graph
5. [[Folders and Hubs]] — structure that scales past 50 notes
6. [[Working with AI Agents]] — the reason this cockpit exists
7. [[Using Obsidian (Optional)]] — a comfortable editor, when you want one
8. [[Bắt Đầu — Tiếng Việt]] — tóm tắt tiếng Việt
